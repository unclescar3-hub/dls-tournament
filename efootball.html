eFootball Match Rules

1. Match Setup (Friend Match)

  • Create a Match Room and share the Room No. with your opponent via WhatsApp.
  • Match Time: Standard (6 mins/half). Extra Time & Penalties: ON.

2. Disconnections

  • If the eFootball server drops a player, the player remaining in the match takes a 3-0 default win. Ensure your connection is stable!

3. Reporting Scores

  • The winner MUST screenshot the final match result screen showing both Usernames and the score.
  • Submit the score via the Challonge bracket or WhatsApp to the admin.
