Official Tournament Rules

Read carefully. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse.

1. Match Setup & Fixtures

  • Players will receive their opponent's WhatsApp number to coordinate the match. The player listed on the top of the bracket hosts the friendly match.
  • You have exactly 15 minutes from the scheduled start time to join. Failure to respond or join results in an automatic forfeit.

2. Disconnections & Lag

  • If a player disconnects within the first 10 in-game minutes while the score is 0-0, the match will be restarted.
  • If a player disconnects after 10 in-game minutes, the remaining player takes an automatic 3-0 win. Make sure you have a stable network!

3. Reporting Scores

  • MANDATORY: The winner MUST take a screenshot of the final match result screen showing both manager names and the score.
  • Send the screenshot directly to the admin via WhatsApp. Without a valid screenshot, the match may be declared void or require a rematch.

4. Payouts

  • Prize money will be transferred directly to the winners' bank accounts within 24 hours of the final match concluding.
