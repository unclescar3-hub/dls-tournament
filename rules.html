Official Rulebook

Terms, Conditions & Anti-Cheat Protocol

1. The "No Scammer" Match Protocol

To protect the integrity of Unclescar Tournaments and ensure payouts go to the true winners, the following proof-of-win rules are strictly enforced:

  • Video Proof is Mandatory: Screenshots can be faked in minutes. The winning player MUST take a 5 to 10-second screen recording of the final match stats showing both usernames clearly.
  • Dispute Resolution: If a match result is contested and neither player can provide video proof, both players will be disqualified, or the Admin may force a rematch at their discretion.
  • The Admin is Final: The Tournament Admin holds the absolute final say in all disputes, disqualifications, and rulings. Arguing with the Admin after a final ruling will result in a permanent ban from the platform.

2. Payments & Refunds

  • Strictly No Refunds: All entry fees are 100% final. Once the bracket has been generated and your name is on it, no refunds will be issued for no-shows, lag, or connection issues.
  • Skill-Based Competition: Unclescar Tournaments are competitive eSports events based entirely on player skill. This is a tournament of ability, not gambling, betting, or a game of chance.
  • Prize Payouts: Payouts are sent directly to the winner's designated bank account within 24 hours of the tournament's conclusion and verification of results.

3. Connection & Lag

  • It is the sole responsibility of the player to ensure a stable internet connection before entering a match.
  • If a player disconnects during a match, it counts as an automatic forfeit (3-0 loss) unless both players agree to a rematch. Proof of agreement must be submitted to the Admin.
